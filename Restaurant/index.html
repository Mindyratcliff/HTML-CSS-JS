At Puddington's House of Pudding every day is a great day for pudding. We offer over 300 varieties of pudding, ranging from savory to sweet. Our signature Voluptuous Volcano Pudding is not to be missed, with its spicy cayenne drizzle and mountain of dark chocolate mixed with real volcanic ash imported daily from Mt. St. Custard of the Pastry Isles. In the mood for something savory? Try our Sizzling Steak and Kidney Pudding, with fine pork kidney and Black Angus Steak, mixed with aromatic rosemary and earthy nutmeg. Our fine puddings have roots all over the world. At Puddington's, every meal should be pudding.
